Share your back-to-school advice and photos

What does back to school look like for your family this year? Amidst a global pandemic, it can mean something different for every family.

For some, it's still time to organize school supplies, pack lunches, and arrange extracurricular activities — with a few modifications of course, as some kids get ready for a full-time return to the classroom. For others, it's about juggling work-from-home schedules around online math classes, managing Zoom meetups, and teaching kids how to type so they can participate virtually. And in many cases, it's somewhere in between.

CBC Vancouver celebrates diversity in arts and culture

CBC Vancouver is proud to sponsor the Virtual Surrey Fusion Festival – an online celebration of culture, food and music.

The free festival will kick off on Sept. 26 with a 90-minute family-friendly live stream featuring cultural performances, musical entertainment, art lessons, Surrey history, Indigenous education, dance lessons, cooking segments, kids' activities and more.
